ABOUT US

Artplatt.com is a worldwide ecosystem for Artists, Artplaces and everyone interested in Art of all it's various forms. Our passion is Art. Our Mission is to create a simplified and global access to all art-related activities for everyone.

Based on a social network with special art relevant functions it is possible to register as an User to follow or get in touch with the latest trends or most interesting art and artists, or as an Artist to share, to present or to market your works and to get in contact with other Artists, Artplaces and fans. Also you can register as an art-related location or company as an Artplace and use all our benefits for free.

All registrated Artists and Artplaces are shown on our interactive worldmap (Artworld), in which the Artists are only visible for registrated users and Artplaces are visible for non-registrated viewers to.

Artworld shows up besides the worldwide useractivity in general, all Artplaces on their address. That way Art, Artists and Artplaces can be found and contacted more easily regional and global.
